Intelligent Smart City Platform

An integrated IoT + AI platform that transforms Solapur's traffic and parking into an intelligent, citizen-friendly system.

How It Works

1

IoT Sensor Network

432 sensors across all major junctions, parking zones, and arterial roads collect real-time vehicle count, speed, and density data.

2

Machine learning algorithms analyze traffic patterns and dynamically adjust signal timings. Emergency vehicles get instant green corridors.

Smart Parking

Ultrasonic sensors in 400 parking slots provide real-time availability. Citizens book slots from mobile app with QR tickets.

Web and mobile portal for real-time alerts, route planning, issue reporting, and parking bookings. Admin control room for operators.
